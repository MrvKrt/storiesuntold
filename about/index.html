About Me?

Posted on Nov 5, 2023

Who am I? Nobody, really.

Someone you pass on the road. Someone you have to live with in this world we can’t escape from. A friend to some or an enemy to some.

Someone you don’t want to remember or someone you wish you knew more? I may be, but I may not be. So don’t worry about it too much.

On another birthday eve, with a million thoughts in my head, I opened this secret blog to say that I was here, to see how the years have changed me.

You can find me as MrvKrt in this bottomless realm but if you found this secret place, congratulations.
